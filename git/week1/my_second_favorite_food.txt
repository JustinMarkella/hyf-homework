Baked duck legs in mustard honey sauce in the oven
Ingredients
1 lemon, unwaxed
4 tbsp honey, runny
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tbsp mustard, in seeds
1 tbsp soy sauce
½ tsp ground cumin
4 duck legs
2 tbsp vegetable oil
Salt, to taste
Black pepper, to taste


Instructions
Wash the lemon and use a fine grater to grate the zest into a small bowl.
Add the honey, both types of mustard, soy sauce and cumin and mix together.
Preheat the oven to 170°C.
Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan over a medium heat .
Wash the duck legs and sprinkle with salt and pepper on all sides.
Then fry for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
Then brush each duck leg with the sauce, wrap in aluminum foil and place in a casserole dish.
Bake the legs in a preheated oven at 170° C for about 1.5 hours.
